Why founders get stuck

Starting a nonprofit should not feel like solving a puzzle alone.

Most first-time founders are not short on passion. They are short on clear, organized guidance that explains what to do first, what comes next, and how the pieces fit together.

01

Too much conflicting information

Online advice can be scattered, outdated, or written in language that is difficult to apply.

02

Fear of making a costly mistake

03

No clear order of operations
